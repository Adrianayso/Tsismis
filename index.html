Ano ang Tsismis?
Latest happenings sa Pilipinas 🇵🇭
Hintay muna, naglo-load ang news...
🇵🇭 Made in the Philippines
Balita na gets mo talaga.

Tsismis gets the latest Philippine news from trusted local sources and uses AI to break it down in simple Taglish — so you actually understand what's happening in your own country without feeling overwhelmed.

Kasi let's be real — a lot of Filipino news is either too formal, too biased, or just straight up mahirap intindihin. Tsismis fixes that. Every article comes with an AI summary that explains the story — casual, clear, and actually helpful.

Hindi ko pinapalitan ang tunay na journalism. Ginagawa ito upang mas maipadali para sa mga mamayang pilipino na mag-alala sa kung ano ang nangyayari sa ating bansa.
